Wayve, Uber and Nissan today announced the signing of a memorandum of understanding to collaborate and commence activities to realize the deployment of robotaxi services, targeting a pilot deployment in Tokyo by late 2026 using the Nissan LEAF, powered by the Wayve AI Driver and available to riders through Uber.
